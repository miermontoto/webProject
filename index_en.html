Otros Mundos, Otros Soles (2001) fourth album of the mierense project SOMNIUM plane music. The album is a collection of different textures and sounds representing a journey through the Earth and the unknown space.

It is difficult to define the style. All the songs have a very ambient, which allows the listener to freely interpret your intention and message. The group defines it as electrosynthphonic.

The album was released independently in 2001 on CD, but Today there is not much information left about said release. Instead, the group has twice re-released the album separately. digital, one in 2015 through his Bandcamp page, and another very recently through an Argentine label named Cyclical Dreams.
